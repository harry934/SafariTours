Maasai Mara Migration Safari

3 Days / 2 Nights Kenya Small Group

Experience the Wild

Witness the world-famous Great Wildebeest Migration in the Maasai Mara. This 3-day safari offers exceptional game viewing opportunities including the Big Five (Lion, Leopard, Elephant, Buffalo, Rhino). You'll stay in premium lodges located right in the heart of the action, enjoying morning and evening game drives guided by our expert trackers.

Itinerary

1

Nairobi to Maasai Mara

Depart Nairobi early morning. Enjoy a scenic drive through the Great Rift Valley. Arrive at Maasai Mara in time for lunch. Afternoon game drive in search of the Big Five.

2

Full Day Game Drive

Spend the entire day exploring the vast plains. Enjoy a picnic lunch by the Mara River, watching hippos and crocodiles. Optional visit to a Maasai Village in the evening.

3

Morning Drive & Return

Early morning game drive to catch predators on the prowl. Breakfast at the lodge, then depart for Nairobi, arriving in the late afternoon.
