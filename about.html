Our Story

Founded in 2015, Harrysa Safari Tours began with a simple passion: to share the breathtaking beauty of East Africa with the world. What started as a small family-run operation has grown into a premier safari company, but our core values remain the same.

Our Mission

To provide unforgettable, eco-conscious wildlife experiences that inspire a deep appreciation for nature.

Our Vision

To be the leading sustainable safari operator in East Africa, setting the standard for conservation-focused tourism.

Our Values

Integrity, Safety, Conservation, and Excellence in every service we provide to our guests.
